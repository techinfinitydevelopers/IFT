About IFT

India's Launchpad for
Young Entrepreneurs

Children are a powerhouse of ideas! But they lack the right platforms to express creativity, solve real problems, and showcase their potential. India's Future Tycoons (IFT) bridges this gap for students between the ages 13-18 through this mentorship, founder bootcamps, and a national pitch stage, helping students build solutions, gain innovation confidence, earn recognition, access funding, and unlock global opportunities.

Frequently Asked Questions

IFT is India's first-ever entrepreneurship search & mentorship platform for young adults (students). It allows students to showcase ideas, get expert mentorship, and refine ventures that solve real-world problems.
Schools can fill in the form above & our team will get in touch with your team.
Students aged 13–18 years from any school in India can register and submit their ideas, either individually or as teams.
Participation in IFT is completely FREE for students.
IFT is carried out in multiple levels of learning and evaluation:
  • Idea submission & shortlisting
  • Interactive learning sessions
  • Mentorship and refinement of ideas
  • Residential boot camps
  • Final pitch showcase to judges
Top ideas of the year are invited to a 3-day residential bootcamp where:
  • Students get 1-on-1 mentorship from industry experts & entrepreneurs
  • They prepare for the grand finale pitch
  • They compete for prizes, recognition, and investor attention
  • Cash prizes & awards
  • Mentorship and networking opportunities with expert judges
  • Possibilities of seed funding for their venture ideas
  • Exposure on national & international platforms such as NFTE and UNGCNI events
No, it's more than a contest. IFT is an experiential learning journey that builds an entrepreneurial mindset and real-world problem-solving skills, not just idea pitching.
You can register or get more details on the official platform dedicated to IFT — India's Future Tycoons, via ENpower's website.
IFT helps participants in building life skills like creativity, critical thinking, collaboration, and leadership, and learn to convert problems into solutions.
