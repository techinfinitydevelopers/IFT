Competition Rules

Team Formation

  • Each team can have a maximum of 3 members including the leader.
  • One team member must be designated as the Team Leader.
  • A student can only be part of one team per season.

Idea Submission

  • Each team may submit only one idea per season.
  • Submitted ideas must be original and not plagiarised.
  • Ideas must address a real-world problem with a viable solution.

Evaluation & Judging

  • Ideas are evaluated on Innovation, Feasibility, Impact, and Presentation.
  • Scores are final once the evaluation window closes.
  • Top teams from each region qualify for the national finale.
